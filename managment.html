What is Smart Waste Managment ?

Smart waste management refers to the application of advanced technologies and innovative strategies to optimize the collection, transportation, disposal, and recycling of waste materials. It leverages various digital tools, sensors, data analytics, and communication systems to improve efficiency, reduce costs, minimize environmental impact, and enhance overall sustainability in waste management processes. Smart waste management is a tech-driven approach to handling trash collection and disposal. It leverages technologies like the Internet of Things (IoT) to collect data on waste levels, sorting, and even generation patterns. This data is then used to optimize waste collection routes, reduce unnecessary pickups, and promote recycling.
